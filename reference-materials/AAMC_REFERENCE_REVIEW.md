# AAMC PREview Reference Review

This project reviewed the official AAMC PREview preparation page and the free
practice-exam PDFs linked from AAMC as structural reference only. No official
scenarios, answer choices, rationales, or answer keys are reproduced in this app.

Observed high-level patterns used for calibration:

- Scenarios are short, realistic, and focused on judgment rather than medical
  knowledge.
- Each scenario presents several independent actions to rate on a four-point
  effectiveness scale.
- Strong responses tend to be timely, honest, respectful, accountable, and
  practical.
- Mid-level responses often have a reasonable intent but are incomplete,
  indirect, poorly timed, or missing follow-through.
- Weak responses tend to avoid responsibility, damage trust, breach privacy,
  escalate inappropriately, or ignore the central concern.
- Rationales are concise and explain why a rating is more appropriate than a
  neighboring rating.

Developer note: continue using official materials only as private calibration
reference. Do not copy, paraphrase closely, or embed official AAMC content.
